Paris, France


Paris, often called the "City of Light", is one of the most famous holiday destinations in the world. Known for its romantic atmosphere, stunning architecture, and rich history, Paris offers an unforgettable experience for travelers of all kinds.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A historic Gothic church with breathtaking design.
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous for shopping, dining, and the Arc de Triomphe.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A scenic way to explore the city’s landmarks.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Buttery, flaky pastries best enjoyed with coffee.
  • Baguette – A classic French bread that is crisp on the outside and soft inside.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate sweets filled with flavored cream.
  • Escargots – A traditional French delicacy of snails cooked in garlic butter.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory.
